The Challenge

When people experience symptoms, they often seek immediate answers. However, symptom overlap between diseases makes self-diagnosis confusing. Our objective was to simplify this process by offering a platform where users could input symptoms and receive accurate disease predictions in real time.

Our Approach

The solution was a web app that accepts symptoms and returns a likely diagnosis using a trained machine learning model. Here's how it works:

  1. Symptom Entry: Users input symptoms through an intuitive form. The system recognizes a wide range of symptoms.
  2. Model Prediction: A pre-trained Decision Tree Classifier evaluates the symptoms and predicts the most probable disease.
  3. Immediate Feedback: Users instantly receive results, making the process fast and accessible.
  4. Additional Insights: The app also provides a description of the disease, precautions, suggested medications, dietary tips, and recommended workouts.
